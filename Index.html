Our Innovative Technologies

Recirculating Aquaculture Systems (RAS)

Advanced water-efficient system that minimizes environmental impact by continuously filtering and recycling water.

  • • 90% less water usage compared to traditional methods
  • • Eliminates water discharge into natural ecosystems
  • • Controlled and sustainable fish cultivation

Bio Floc Technology

Innovative method promoting beneficial microorganism growth for improved water quality and fish nutrition.

  • • Reduces external feed requirements
  • • Natural water quality improvement
  • • Cost-effective fish farming approach
